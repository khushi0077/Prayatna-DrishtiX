Ultrasonic Sensors (HC-SR04) - The Eyes

    VCC → ESP32 VIN (5V)

    GND → ESP32 GND

    Trig → ESP32 D5

    Echo → ESP32 D18

    (If using 2 sensors, connect the second one to D19 (Trig) and D21 (Echo))


GSM Module (SIM800L) - The Offline Mouth

    ⚠️ WARNING: SIM800L needs 3.7V-4.2V. Do NOT connect to 5V (burn) or 3.3V (unstable). Use a Li-ion battery or a buck converter.

    VCC → External Battery + (Common Ground with ESP32)

    GND → ESP32 GND

    TX → ESP32 D16 (RX2)

    RX → ESP32 D17 (TX2)
